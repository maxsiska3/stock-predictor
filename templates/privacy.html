Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

What we collect

Kouros stores account information you provide (email, display name, and a hashed password), your watchlists, fund definitions, and position entries. We do not collect payment information.

Third-party market data

Live quotes, search results, and related market fields are retrieved from Yahoo Finance on your behalf when you use the dashboard. Yahoo Finance is operated by Yahoo Finance LLC. Kouros is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Yahoo.

We cache market responses briefly in server memory (typically under two minutes for prices and up to five minutes for search) to reduce load on Yahoo’s services. We do not resell or sublicense Yahoo market data.

How we use data

Account and portfolio data are used only to operate your dashboard — authentication, watchlist management, fund aggregation, and ML-based direction signals. We do not sell your personal information.

Retention

Portfolio data persists in our SQLite database until you delete it or close your account. Cached market data is ephemeral and is not stored long-term.

Your choices

You can remove tickers, funds, and positions at any time from the dashboard. To delete your account entirely, contact the project maintainer.

Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue on the Kouros GitHub repository.
